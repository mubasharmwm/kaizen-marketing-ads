# Brand Brief — Kaizen Marketing Agency
## Generated from website audit: http://kaizen-marketingagency.com/
## Audit date: 2026-03-18

---

## Product
- **Name:** Kaizen Marketing Agency — 400,000 Digital Products with Master Resell Rights
- **What it is:** A one-time purchase giving buyers instant access to 400,000 digital products (courses, PDFs, templates, reels) with Master Resell Rights — they can resell everything and keep 100% of profits
- **Price:** $14 (primary offer) / $19 (alternate funnel) — one-time payment
- **Geographic target:** USA
- **Secondary product:** 100,000 Reels with Master Resell Rights (70% OFF upsell)
- **What the customer gets:** 400,000 digital products + resell rights + ability to keep all profits

---

## Target Buyer
- **Who they are:** Entrepreneurs, side hustlers, and make-money-online beginners who want a low-cost entry point into digital product reselling
- **Their biggest pain:** No product to sell, no time to create one, and no budget for expensive courses
- **What they've tried before:** Dropshipping, affiliate marketing, expensive courses — high effort, low return
- **What they want:** A ready-made business they can launch immediately and keep all the money

---

## Unique Mechanism
- **What makes this different:** Volume + price. 400,000 products for $14 is a category-defining value proposition — no competitor bundles at this scale for this price
- **Why this works:** Buyers don't have to create anything. They get a ready-made inventory and full resell rights instantly

---

## Proof
- Currently converting at 1-2 sales/day organically — proven offer
- No testimonials currently on site
- No case studies or income screenshots
- No guarantees mentioned
- **Opportunity:** Adding social proof would significantly strengthen ad conversions

---

## Brand Voice
- **Tone:** Direct, bold, action-oriented, minimal. No fluff.
- **Words/phrases used:** "Master Resell Rights", "One-Time Payment", "Keep 100% Profits", "Instant Access", "Scale Fast", "Yes, I want all that"
- **Words/phrases avoided:** Technical jargon, corporate language, passive voice
- **Overall feel:** Scrappy but confident. Like a street-smart deal you'd be foolish to pass up.

---

## Visual Identity
- **Background color:** #faf4f1 (cream/beige) — this is the PRIMARY BACKGROUND, NEVER use as text or accent color
- **Primary accent color:** Orange/red — used on CTA buttons ONLY, never as background
- **Text color:** Dark (near black)
- **Secondary colors:** Not confirmed — site is minimal
- **Heading fonts:** Gilda Display, Space Grotesk, Josefin Sans
- **Body font:** Inter
- **Imagery style:** Product screenshot gallery — dense grid of product mockups/thumbnails, no lifestyle photography
- **Layout:** Centered, modular, image-heavy, minimal copy, mobile-optimized
- **Ad dimensions:** 1080x1080 (product grid aesthetic) or 1080x1350 (for mobile-first)
- **Color inversion warning:** The background is ALWAYS #faf4f1 (cream). The accent/CTA color is orange. DO NOT use orange as the background in any generated ad image. DO NOT use cream as a CTA or accent color.

---

## Brand Rules (Ad Copy)
- **Approved brand name phrasings:** "Kaizen Marketing Agency", "Kaizen Marketing"
- **Words to never use in ads:** "get rich quick", "guaranteed income", "make millions" — keep it grounded
- **Pricing in ads:** YES — $14 price point is a core hook, lead with it
- **Competitor mentions:** None — position by volume/value, not against competitors
- **Ad formats in scope:** Static images (product grid), short-form video/reels (given 100K reels product)
- **Timeline claims:** "Instant access" is approved and accurate
- **Other restrictions:** Facebook disclaimer required on all ads (already on site)
