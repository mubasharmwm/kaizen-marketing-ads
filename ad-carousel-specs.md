## Visual Continuity Rules (All Carousels)
- Background: #faf4f1 cream on every card — no exceptions
- Font system: Space Grotesk Bold for headlines, Gilda Display Italic for emotional lines, Inter for body
- Accent: #E8581A orange for arrows, CTAs, and emphasis only
- Swipe indicator: thin orange "›" chevron, bottom-right of all non-final cards
- Card size: 1080x1080 each
- Transition style: Left-to-right narrative momentum — each card must answer "what's next?"

## Card Narrative Structure (All Carousels)
- Card 1: HOOK — stop the scroll, create curiosity or recognition
- Card 2-3: BUILD — reveal, compare, or demonstrate
- Card N-1: PROOF or MECHANISM — the logical support
- Final Card: CTA — single clear action, link in bio

## Facebook Carousel Technical Specs
- Max cards: 10 (we use 4-5)
- Image ratio: 1:1 (1080x1080)
- File format: JPG or PNG
- Max file size: 30MB per card
- Headline (below card): 40 characters max
- Description (below headline): 20 characters max
- Primary text (top of carousel): 125 characters recommended, 500 max
